ℹ️ About the SK
Know your Sangguniang Kabataan
🏛️
What is the SK?
The Sangguniang Kabataan (SK) is the official youth governing body in every barangay in the Philippines. It is composed of elected youth officials aged 18 to 24 who represent and serve the youth sector of the community under the Local Government Code and Republic Act 10742 (SK Reform Law).
🎯
Our Mission
To empower the youth of Brgy. Tula Tula Grande by providing meaningful programs, activities, and services that promote education, livelihood, health, sports, and civic engagement. We strive to be the voice of the youth in local governance and community development.
👁️
Our Vision
A Brgy. Tula Tula Grande where every youth is empowered, engaged, and equipped — contributing to a progressive, peaceful, and prosperous community. We envision a generation of responsible, capable, and patriotic young leaders.
⚖️
Legal Basis
The SK operates under Republic Act 10742 (SK Reform Law of 2015) and its implementing rules. The SK Chairperson sits as an ex-officio member of the Sangguniang Barangay, ensuring youth representation in barangay legislation.
📜 Know Your Youth Rights
Rights of every Filipino youth under the law
  • Right to relevant quality education, including alternative and non-formal education
  • Right to gainful employment and equitable compensation
  • Right to live in, and have access to, a safe, clean, and sustainable environment
  • Right to health, including access to reproductive health services and information
  • Right to physical and psychological safety from all forms of abuse and exploitation
  • Right to participate in civic, political, cultural, and sports activities
  • Right to be heard in the community, including in SK governance and local legislation
  • Right to free legal assistance and access to justice
  • Right to express views and opinions on matters affecting youth
  • Right to a balanced and healthy diet, especially for out-of-school youth and those in poverty

🌟 SK Programs & Services
What the SK offers to the youth of Tula Tula Grande
📚
Education & Training
Scholarship assistance, leadership seminars, life skills training, and alternative learning support for out-of-school youth.
💼
Livelihood Programs
Entrepreneurship workshops, skills training, livelihood assistance, and job-matching support for youth seeking employment.
⚽
Sports & Recreation
Inter-barangay sports tournaments, sports equipment assistance, and recreational activities for youth health and wellness.
🌿
Environment & Community
Clean-up drives, tree-planting activities, community development projects, and environmental awareness campaigns.
